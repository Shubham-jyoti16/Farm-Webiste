Visitor information


Visitor Guidlines

• All visitors to Frankston Farm must wear enclosed shoes

• Visitors are encouraged to bring water and wear a hat.

• Visitors may not bring pets or other animals to the farm.

• COVID-19 vaccination is a requirement of entry to the farm in line with the public health direction. Visitors who are 16 years of age or older must provide proof of vaccination or medical exemption using the Check in Queensland app or another form of record-keeping before entering the farm.

• Visitors are welcome to bring their own food to the farm to enjoy a picnic lunch. The snack bar is open daily and sells burgers, hot dogs, sandwiches, hot chips, coffee, tea, juice, soft drinks, icecream, and confectionary. The snack bar also sells Frankston Farm gift cards, which are valid for 12 months from the date of purchase.
