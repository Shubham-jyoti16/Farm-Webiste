About us

Frankston Farm is a hobby farm that offers public tours for visitors who would like an authentic farm experience.

The farm was established in 1985 by owner operators Richard and Lynn Frankston. The farm has horses, cattle, donkeys, sheep, goats, alpacas, rabbits, guinea pigs, chickens, ducks and pigs
